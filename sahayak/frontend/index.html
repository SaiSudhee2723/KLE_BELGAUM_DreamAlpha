Everything you need

Clinical intelligence.
Built for the field.

Every feature of Sahayak AI is designed to save time, reduce errors, and protect lives — for both patients and healthcare workers.

Voice-First, 10 Languages
Speak symptoms in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali and 4 more. Whisper AI transcribes locally on AMD NPU.
100% Offline
ICMR / WHO RAG Engine
Symptoms cross-referenced against 12 disease protocols stored locally using FAISS vector search. No cloud required.
Structured Clinical Triage
Instant risk level (Emergency/High/Medium/Low), confidence score, action items, and warning signs for every patient.
AI Report Extraction (OCR)
Upload any PDF, X-ray, blood test, or ECG report. LLaMA 70B automatically extracts all vitals, lab values, and history.
AI-Generated Referral Reports
Doctor-quality PDF reports with full history, charts, comparisons, medications. Download and share instantly.
Secure Doctor Sharing
Share your complete health history with any doctor via a one-time code. Doctor gets read-only access instantly.
