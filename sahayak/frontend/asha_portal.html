All diagnosis records, patient vitals, and outbreak alerts are stored in local SQLite on this device. When you tap "Sync Now", only new unsynced records are pushed to the Karnataka Government Supabase DB. If network drops mid-sync, data stays safely queued locally and retries automatically.
